Case 01 / Fintech

Helios

A consumer fintech app that turns everyday spending into effortless, confident money decisions.

Role
Lead Product Designer
Timeline
14 weeks · 2025
Team
3 designers · 6 engineers
Platform
iOS · Android
The Challenge

Users abandoned onboarding before connecting an account. The flow demanded too much, too soon, and the value of the product never landed before the first ask.

The Approach

Eight rounds of usability testing reframed onboarding around a single first win. I rebuilt the information architecture and a token-driven component library to ship it fast.

The Outcome

Activation rose and time-to-first-value dropped by more than half. The system now powers every surface across both platforms.

Process & Screens
What I'd carry forward

The biggest lever wasn't a screen — it was deciding what to remove. Earning trust before asking for it changed every metric that mattered.
